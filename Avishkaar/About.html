About REC Azamgarh

Rajkiya Engineering College, Azamgarh was established in the year 2010. The college was established by the Government of Uttar Pradesh and was formerly known as Manyawar Kanshiram Engineering College of Information Technology. The campus is spread over 14 acres of land. REC, Azamgarh is a public co-educational institute. REC, Azamgarh is approved by the AICTE. The college has a total of 26 faculty members and 775 students. REC, Azamgarh is a constituent college of Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University, Uttar Pradesh. Facilities such as hostel, gym, library, sports, cafeteria, laboratories, etc are provided by the REC, Azamgarh. The college offers undergraduate B.Tech courses in the field of Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Information Technology. Placement and scholarship opportunities are also provided by the Rajkiya Engineering College, Azamgarh.

Rajkiya Engineering College Location

REC, Azamgarh is located at Akbalpur, Post, Vill, Deogaon, Uttar Pradesh. Ganguli Railway Station is 15.5 km away from the college and it will take around 41 minutes to reach the campus. Lal Bahadur Shastri International Airport is around 58.1 km away from the college and Lalganj Bus Stand is 11 km away from the institute. Students can take an auto or hire a taxi to reach there.
